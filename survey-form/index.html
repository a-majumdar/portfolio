Dear Participant,

Thank you for agreeing to participate in our study aimed at assessing the effectiveness of an educational tool focusing on specific concepts in number theory. Please take your time to answer the questions thoughtfully and honestly. Your feedback will provide valuable insights that will help us improve the educational tool and enhance the learning experience for future users.
